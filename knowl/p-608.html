An electrically powered conveyor belt 10 m long is tilted and used to lift bricks to the top of a building 5 m high. The belt will hold 100 bricks of mass 2 kg and makes one revolution every minute.
  1. How long does it take to transport a given brick from the bottom of the belt to the top?
  2. What is the minimum electrical power required to run the conveyor belt motor? (Also express answer in horsepower.)
  3. What is the minimum force required to move the bricks up the incline?
  4. If the coefficient of friction between belt and the surface that supports it is 0.3, what is the actual force required to move the bricks?
  5. What is the AMA of this machine?
  6. What is the IMA of this machine?
  7. What is the efficiency of this machine?
  8. What is the actual power required to operate the machine?
