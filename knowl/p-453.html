A heavy rope of weight w is hung between two hooks at equal heights. The weight is distributed uniformly along the rope as it sags between the two hooks. The angle that the rope makes with the horizontal at the left hook is \(\theta\text{.}\)
  1. In terms of $w$ and \(\theta\text{,}\) what is the angle that the rope makes with the horizontal at the right hook? Why?
  2. What is the tension in the rope at the lowest point of the sagging rope?
  3. With what magnitude force does the rope pull on each of the hooks?
