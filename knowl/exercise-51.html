Exercise 2.

A boy uses a sling to throw a rock a distance of 100 m. If the rock is in the air for 5 s, find each of the following:
  1. How fast is the rock initially traveling horizontally?
  2. How fast is the rock initially traveling vertically?
  3. How high does the rock get?
  4. How fast does it hit the ground?
  5. At what angle does it hit the ground?
