It defines the temperature and states the thermal equilibrium condition. Thermal equilibrium is a state achieved by two or more systems after being in contact with one another through a diathermic wall. If an object with a higher temperature comes in contact with another object at a lower temperature, it will transfer some of its heat energy to the object at lower temperature till they come to the same temperature. If two systems are each in thermal equilibrium with a third system, then they must be in thermal equilibrium with each other.
