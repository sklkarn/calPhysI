The value of \(g\) on the surface of the earth does not only vary with the radius of the earth but it is also affected by the rotation of the earth. Any mass rotating in a circular motion needs a centripetal force which is acting towards the center of the circular path. Remember that a centripetal force is not a real force that is acting on a rotating body, but it is the required force that must be applied to keep the body in that rotational motion.
