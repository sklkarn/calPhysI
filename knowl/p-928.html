\begin{equation*} \frac{F}{A} =\sigma \frac{\Delta x}{y} \end{equation*}
where \(\sigma \) is called modulus of shape or shear modulus. Hence
\begin{equation*} \sigma = \frac{\frac{F}{A}}{\frac{\Delta x}{y}} =\frac{\frac{F}{A}}{\theta} =\frac{F}{A\theta} \end{equation*}
Here \(F\) is a deforming force, \(A\) is surface area of a cube, and \(\theta \) is a shear angle.
