A tire of mass 30 kg, radius of gyration 20 cm, and diameter 50 cm accelerates uniformly from rest. A small nail is embedded in the outer tread of the tire. After 100 revolutions the tire is rotating at 400 rpm. At that time, find each of the following:
  1. What is its angular acceleration?
  2. How much time did it take to get up to speed?
  3. What is the speed of the nail?
  4. What is the acceleration of the nail?
  5. What is the moment of inertia of the wheel?
  6. What torque is required to make the wheel rotate?
  7. How much energy was imparted to the wheel?
  8. How much power is required to rotate the wheel?
  9. What is the angular momentum of the wheel?
