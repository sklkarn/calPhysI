Exercise 24.

A particle is moving according to the equation
\begin{equation*} \vec{r} = (A t + B t^{2})\hat{i} + C t^{3}\hat{j}, \end{equation*}
where \(A = 10 \,m/s,\quad B = 2 \,m/s^{2},\) and \(C = - 1 \,m/s^{3}\text{.}\) Find:
  1. \(\displaystyle \vec{r}(0),\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \vec{r}(2 \,s),\)
  3. \(\displaystyle v,\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \vec{c}(-1 \,s),\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \vec{v}(3 \,s),\)
  6. \(\displaystyle a,\)
  7. \(\displaystyle \vec{a}(0.5 \,s).\)
