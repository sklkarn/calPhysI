A robber takes a chest full of gold put it on their horse and ride off into the distance. The size of the chest is about 18 inches long, 10 inches wide, and 8 inches high.
  1. How many pounds would such a chest weigh if it were filled with gold (\(\rho = 19.3 g/ml\))?
  2. What would be the buoyant force (in pounds) on the chest if it were placed under water?
  3. What would be the apparent weight (in pounds) of the chest while under water?
