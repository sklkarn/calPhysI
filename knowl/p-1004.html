Given: \(\rho_{o}=62.43 \,lb/ft^{3},\quad V = lbh=(1.5 \,ft) (0.67 \,ft) (0.83 \,ft) =0.834 \,ft^{3},\) \(\rho_{g} = 19.3 \,g/ml = 19.3 \times 62.43 \,lb/ft^{3} = 1205 \,lb/ft^{3}.\)
  1. \begin{equation*} W=mg=V_{c}\rho_{g} g \end{equation*}
    \begin{equation*} = 0.834 ft^{3}\times 1205 lb/ft^{3}\times 32ft/s^{2} = 32159 \,pdl \quad (or, poundal) \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} B= wt. loss =V_{c} \rho_{o} g = 0.834 ft^{3}\times 62.43 lb/ft^{3}\times 32ft/s^{2}=1665 \,pdl \end{equation*}
  3. \begin{equation*} B=w-w'\quad \Rightarrow\quad w'=w-B =32159-1665 =30494 \,pdl \end{equation*}
