Equations of motion in a circular motion:
\begin{equation} \omega_{f}=\omega_{i}+\alpha t \tag{3.4.15} \end{equation}
\begin{equation} \omega^{2}_{f}=\omega^{2}_{i}+2\alpha\theta \tag{3.4.16} \end{equation}
\begin{equation} \theta = \omega_{i}t+\frac{1}{2}\alpha t^{2} \tag{3.4.17} \end{equation}
