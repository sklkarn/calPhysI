Exercise 26.

A hang glider is flying in a horizontal circle of radius 20 m, completing the circle in a time period of 10 s.
  1. What is the speed of the glider?
  2. What is the tangential acceleration of the glider?
  3. What is the radial acceleration of the glider?
  4. Express this last answer in g’s of acceleration.
  5. How many g’s of force would the pilot be experiencing? Would this be noticeable?
