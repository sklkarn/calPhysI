The force from surface tension is \(F = 2\gamma l,\) where the factor 2 is taken because of two surfaces of the soap bubble.
\begin{equation*} \therefore F = 2\gamma 2\pi r = 4\gamma \pi r \end{equation*}
The force do to the pressure difference must balance the force from the surface tension. The force to the pressure difference is
\begin{equation*} F = pA= (p_{i} - p_{o})(\pi r^{2}) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad 4\gamma \pi r = (p_{i} - p_{o})(\pi r^{2}) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad (\Delta p) = \frac{4\gamma}{r} \end{equation*}
