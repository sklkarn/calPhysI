Kirchhoff has two fundamental principles in the study of thermal radiation and the behavior of materials with regard to electromagnetic radiation. The details of Kirchhoff’s law is not a part of discussion here but only taken here for some information. Kirchhoff’s first law states that at any temperature, the ratio of the emissive power to the absorptive power for a given wavelength is the same for all bodies and is equal to the emissive power of a perfectly black body.
