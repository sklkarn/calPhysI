An asteroid 1 mi in diameter of density \(4 g/cm^{3}\) enters the earth’s gravity when it is at rest with respect to the solar system.
  1. What is the speed of the asteroid with respect to the earth before entering the earth’s gravity?
  2. What is the speed of the asteroid when it hits the surface of the earth?
  3. What is the energy of the asteroid when it hits the earth’s surface?
  4. How does this compare with the amount of solar energy which hits the surface of the earth every day if the solar constant is \(1.37 kW/m^{2}\text{?}\)
