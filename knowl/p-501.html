A 60 kg man and his 40 kg dog are sitting together at the left end of a 8.0 m long boat. The boat is 100 kg, and its center of mass lies at the geometric center. The boat starts out at rest in the middle of a calm lake. Ignore all friction and water resistance throughout this problem.
  1. Where is the center of mass of the boat + man + dog system? Consider the origin of coordinates lies at left end of the boat.
  2. Suppose that the dog moves to the other end of the boat, while the man stays still. How far does the boat move and in which direction? (left or right).
  3. After reaching the other end of the boat, the dog jumps off with a horizontal velocity of 2.0 m/s, directed to the right, as measured relative to the water. What is the speed with which the boat and the man drift in the opposite direction?
