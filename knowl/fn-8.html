The advanced way of finding the solution of such type of differential equation is to find the nature of roots \(\alpha\pm i\beta \) of the equation and then the solution can be given by \(x=Ae^{\alpha t}[\sin(\beta t+\gamma)] \text{.}\) Where \(A\) and \(\gamma\) are arbitrary constants, and \(\alpha\) and \(\beta\) are roots of the auxiliary equation.
