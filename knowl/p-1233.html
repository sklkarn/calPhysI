The work done \(W_{2}\) in this case is given by
\begin{equation*} W_{2} = \int\limits_{v_{2}}^{v_{3}}p\,dV = \int\limits_{v_{2}}^{v_{3}}k\frac{\,dV}{V^{\gamma}} \end{equation*}
\([\because \quad pV^{\gamma}=constant, (k)]\) for adiabatic process.
\begin{equation*} =k\left(\frac{V^{1-\gamma}}{1-\gamma}\right)_{v_{2}}^{v_{3}} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} =\frac{k}{1-\gamma} \left(V_{3}^{1-\gamma}-V_{2}^{1-\gamma}\right) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} = \frac{1}{1-\gamma} \left(p_{3}V_{3}^{\gamma}V_{3}^{1-\gamma}-p_{2}V_{2}^{\gamma}V_{2}^{1-\gamma}\right) \end{equation*}
\([\because \quad p_{2}V_{2}^{\gamma}=p_{3}V_{3}^{\gamma}=k]\)
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad W_{2} = \frac{1}{1-\gamma}\left(p_{3}V_{3}-p_{2}V_{2}\right) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} =\frac{\left(RT_{c}-RT_{h}\right)}{1-\gamma}=\frac{R}{\gamma-1}\left(T_{h}-T_{c}\right) = area BCcb \end{equation*}
