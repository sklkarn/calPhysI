Exercise 42.

A Carnot engine in one cycle takes in 2000 J of heat at 500 K and discards heat at 300 K.
  1. How much work does it do?
  2. How much heat does it discard?
  3. What is its change in entropy during its isothermal expansion?
  4. What is its change in entropy during its adiabatic expansion?
  5. What is its change in entropy during its isothermal compression?
  6. What is its change in entropy during its adiabatic compression?
  7. What is its total change in entropy?
  8. What is the change in entropy of the hot reservoir?
  9. What is the change in entropy of the cold reservoir?
  10. What is the change in entropy of the universe?
  11. Is this result consistent with the second law of thermodynamics?
