Exercise 29.

  1. A force of 1000 N is applied to an object that travels a distance of 10 m in 3 s. What is the impulse of the force?
  2. A force rises linearly from zero to 106 Newtons in two milliseconds and then decreases linearly back to zero in the next two milliseconds. What is the total impulse of that force.
  3. A force is described mathematically by the equation \(F = F_{o}[1 - (t - 1)^{2}]\text{,}\) where \(F_{o} = 100 N\) and t is expressed in seconds and ranges from zero to 2 seconds. What is the impulse of this force?
