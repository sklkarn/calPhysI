Exercise 20.

At the instant that the traffic light turns green, a car starting from rest with an acceleration of \(2.50 m/s^{2}\) is passed by a truck moving at a constant velocity of 60.0 km/hr.
  1. How long will it take for the car to overtake the truck?
  2. How far from the starting point will the car overtake the truck?
  3. At what velocity will the car be moving when it overtakes the truck?
