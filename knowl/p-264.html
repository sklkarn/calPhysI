A particle is moving according to the equation
\begin{equation*} \vec{r}=\left(At^{3}+Bt^{2}\right)\hat{i}+Ct\hat{j} \end{equation*}
where \(A= 3m/s^{2} \text{,}\) \(B= 2 m/s^{2}\text{,}\) and \(C= -1m/s\text{.}\) Find
  1. \(\vec{r}(0)\text{,}\)
  2. \(\vec{r}(2s)\text{,}\)
  3. \(v_{avg}(0,2s)\text{,}\)
  4. \(\vec{v}(-1s)\text{,}\)
  5. \(\vec{v}(3s)\text{,}\)
  6. \(a_{avg}(-1s,3s)\text{,}\) and
  7. \(\vec{a}(0.5s)\text{.}\)
